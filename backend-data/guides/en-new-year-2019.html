It's time to take a look back at 2019! In 2019, I spent 173 days traveling - just under half the year. I visited 25 countries, of which 11 new: Australia, New Zealand, Tonga, Fiji, Brunei, Northern Mariana Islands, Guam, Guernsey, Jersey, Ireland, Isle of Man. I visited other 14 countries again: Russia, Belarus, Netherlands, Thailand, Singapore, Indonesia, Malaysia, Hong Kong, Macau, South Korea, United Kingdom, Serbia, France, Switzerland There were only three and a half long-distance trips across Russia this year, but I visited 5 new regions: Bashkiria, Kurgan region, Chelyabinsk region, Khanty-Mansi Autonomous Okrug and Yamalo-Nenets Autonomous Okrug; I also visited the Kostroma and Ivanovo regions again and stopped in the Bryansk region on the way from Belarus. The total number of Russian regions visited reached 70. This year I spent the night in 64 different places while traveling, and, as I counted, there were an equal number of hotels and airbnbs among them - 32 each. Although in terms of total duration, airbnb will have a big lead - I mostly stayed in hotels for 1-2 days. During the year I took a record 55 flights: * Moscow - Minsk * Moscow - Ufa, Chelyabinsk - Moscow * Moscow - Eindhoven - Moscow * Moscow - Bangkok - Singapore - Darwin - Adelaide - Brisbane - Christchurch, Dunedin - Wellington, Auckland - Tonga - Nadi - Suva, Nadi - Sydney, Melbourne - Hobart - Perth - Bali * Bali - Kuala Lumpur - Brunei - Kuala Lumpur - Banda Aceh - Kuala Lumpur - Langkawi - Kuala Lumpur - Hong Kong - Saipan - Guam - Seoul - Irkutsk - Moscow * Moscow - London, Southampton - Guernsey - Jersey - Birmingham, Bristol - Dublin, Belfast - Manchester, Liverpool - Isle of Man - Liverpool, Manchester - Berlin - Moscow * Moscow - London - Edinburgh, London - Moscow * Moscow - Surgut - Moscow * Moscow - Belgrade - Paris - Belgrade - Moscow * Moscow - London - Dublin - London - Moscow I flew on AirAsia 8 times, FlyBe 6 times, Pobeda 5 times, Ryanair, Wizz Air, Jetstar, Virgin Australia and Air Serbia 4 times each. By metro in the cities: Moscow, Minsk, Amsterdam, Bangkok, Singapore, Adelaide, Brisbane, Sydney, Melbourne, Perth, Kuala Lumpur, Hong Kong, Seoul, Incheon, London, Glasgow, Newcastle, Lyon, Paris.
