It's time to take a look back at 2021. It's been a great year in terms of travel! There weren’t many trips, but everything was straight on point - now you can’t go to Europe for a few days (well, can’t you fly through Budapest all the time?), so every time I approach the matter thoroughly. In 2021, I spent 179 days traveling - almost reaching half of the year. I visited 16 countries, of which 9 new: Bolivia, Belize, Guatemala, Salvador, Honduras, Nicaragua, Costa Rica, Panama, Bahamas. I visited other 7 countries again: Russia, Brazil, Belarus, Bulgaria, Romania, USA, Turkey There were almost no trips around Russia this year (only two to St. Petersburg and trips around Moscow without an overnight stay), and there were no new regions either. I've stayed overnight in 61 different places on my travels this year, mostly in hotels; I've only used airbnb four times (Rio de Janeiro, Recife, Washington DC and Miami).
