It's time to look back at 2018! This year I only traveled for about three months in total. The year was dedicated to financial rehabilitation for previous failures, especially in 2017, when I went into a serious red area. With two new jobs, I managed to earn more in 2018 than in the previous three years combined. This year I visited 17 countries, of which 3 new: Oman, Iceland, Canada. I visited 14 countries again: Russia, Germany, Switzerland, France, Estonia, Sweden, Spain (twice), Lithuania (twice), UAE, USA, Poland, Greece, Czech Republic, Slovakia This year I returned to traveling around the regions of Russia (the first trip, however, was at the very end of December 2017, but we can add that too for completeness). After a massive study of Russia in 2013-2014, for three years I went almost nowhere, except for Moscow and St. Petersburg and a couple of other cities passing through. New regions have been added: Tambov, Lipetsk, Penza, Saratov, Rostov, Tomsk, Kemerovo, Kirov regions, Mordovia, Adygea, Udmurtia. I have stayed in 41 hotels (including hostels and airbnbs), in 2 houses. I also spent 10 nights on the road. During the year I took 41 flights - much more than last year, and two less than the record 2014. I traveled by trains (intercity) in the following countries: Russia, Germany, Switzerland, Spain, Lithuania, Greece, Czech Republic, Slovakia. By metro in the cities: Moscow, Stockholm, St. Petersburg, Barcelona, Dubai, Montreal, Toronto, Cleveland, Atlanta, St. Louis, Vancouver, Nizhny Novgorod, Prague. Happy New Year!
