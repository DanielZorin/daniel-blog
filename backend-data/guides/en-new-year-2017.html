It's time to look back at 2017! This year I visited 19 countries, of which 5 new ones: India, Nepal, Bangladesh, Japan, Order of Malta. I have visited 14 countries again: Russia, Finland, Poland, Italy, Vatican, Malta, Germany, Latvia, Estonia, Turkey, Malaysia, Thailand, Greece, Albania I had previously been to Greece in 1997, so it can be considered that this was also my first time. I stayed in 75 hotels (including hostels and airbnb), in 5 houses. Also spent 20 nights on the road: - on trains in Russia, India, Bangladesh and Thailand - on buses in Estonia, Finland, Nepal - five times on a plane - once at the airport. The most expensive hotel was in Italy for 40 euros, in Germany it was about the same. In all other places $25 and below. The cheapest hotels were in Nepal on the track, but there they actually make money as a restaurant. Of the rest, the cheapest was in India for 180 rupees ($3, the city of Jodhpur, by the way, a good hotel, there have been much worse ones in India and at the same time much more expensive). During the year I took 25 flights. I traveled by trains (intercity) in the following countries: Italy, Germany, Russia, India, Bangladesh, Thailand, Japan. By metro in the cities: St. Petersburg, Naples, Rome, Berlin, Hamburg, Moscow, Delhi, Gurgaon, Kolkata, Kuala Lumpur, Cochin, Mumbai, Chennai, Bangalore, Jaipur, Athens, Tokyo, Yokohama, Nagoya, Kyoto, Osaka, Fukuoka.
