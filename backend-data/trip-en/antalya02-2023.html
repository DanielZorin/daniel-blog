Turkey: Antalya

19 February 2023

Antalya

One day I went to Antalya to visit the zoo. It is located on the outskirts of the city near the tram terminus, just five stops from the bus station. There is a residential area around.

On the rock in the distance you can see the monument to Ataturk.

Newly built area.

The final tram station.

Tram depot.

Entrance to the zoo. The ticket costs only 7 liras ($0.40), if by car, then 20 liras.

All sorts of animals in the zoo. Ostrich.

Kangaroo.

Turtles.

Many different types of sheep and goats.

Boar.

Yak.

If you climb to the very top, there will be a panorama of Antalya.

Even higher there is a highway with this head of Ataturk in the rock.

Bear.

Tiger.

Camel.

The pelican amusingly tried to swallow the apple, but was unable to do so, and it floated away into the neighboring enclosure.

Snake in a terrarium.
