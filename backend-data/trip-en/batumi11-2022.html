Poti

Poti is a non-tourist port city in western Georgia, close to the border with Abkhazia. Poti belongs to Megrelia - a region that has its own separate language, related to Georgian, and Mingrelian khachapuri comes from here.

In the central park, a pre-revolutionary cathedral in a neo-Byzantine style, rare for Georgia, has been preserved.
