Sweden: Stockholm

4 December 2016

Stockholm

This was my first time on an overnight cruise! In general, cruises as a way of travel in most cases are inferior in all respects to any other options, even bus tours. On bus tours, at least cities like Rome or Paris are allocated 2-3 days, and not half a day, like on a cruise. With a stretch, cruises can be considered justified for traveling around the Caribbean (if you are not alone); nevertheless, by plane it will be cheaper. But as an option for a short weekend trip, a cruise on the Baltic Sea is very good - for the price it turns out to be the cheapest way to travel from Stockholm to Helsinki or Tallinn. From Tallinn a round trip for a cabin for two costs from 50 euros, from Helsinki - from 35. One day in Stockholm, of course, is not enough if this is your first time. Especially on a cruise from Tallinn, because there is less time on shore. But there is enough time to walk through familiar places.
