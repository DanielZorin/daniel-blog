Balashikha

The city of Balashikha adjoins Moscow from the east. I always thought that it was BalAshikha (probably because of the famous comic video about foreign language courses in Balashikha), but it turned out that BalashIkha. You need to go here by minibus from the Novogireevo metro station, since the train station is not located in the city center, and trains do not go there very often and take a long time, as the route goes along an arc. The price of travel on a minibus was an unpleasant surprise; it was twice as expensive as in Moscow, although the ride there didn’t take long (if you don’t get caught in a traffic jam).
