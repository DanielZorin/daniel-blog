Have you seen enough? Now we can speculate about what the main feature of Paris is, why this city can be called the capital of the world (one of them). The fact is that Paris is the center of everything new and grandiose. For Paris, nothing is sacred, and this, by the way, in a sense makes it similar to Moscow. When Stalin demolished almost all of old Moscow and half of the Kremlin in addition, it was somewhat dangerous to complain about this, so the habit of scolding the authorities for urban planning initiatives appeared under Luzhkov. Paris went through this back in the nineteenth century, when the Eiffel Tower was built.

The Eiffel Tower broke the pattern of Paris at that time, although, it must be said, the city was already relatively new, not medieval.
