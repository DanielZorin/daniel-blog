Monaco

12 March 2014

Monaco is strikingly different from other European microstates (such as Andorra, Liechtenstein, Luxembourg, Vatican or San Marino). This is a fragment of old feudal Europe, but not lost somewhere far away, but instead located on the prestigious Cote d'Azur. The most expensive and prestigious real estate is here, and visually Monaco is most similar to Hong Kong.

Monaco's area is much smaller than even Andorra's, so millionaires are forced to live here in tall apartment buildings, just like on the outskirts of Moscow. Private villas are for billionaires.

The state of Monaco consists of four cities adjacent to each other: Monaco, Monte Carlo, La Condamine and Fontveille. Only Monaco is still an old European city, the rest are being built up.
