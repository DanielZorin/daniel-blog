Turkey: Kemer

11 September 2022 - 10 April 2023

Kemer

In anticipation of the onset of winter in Kemer, I bought a couple of heaters and prepared for the constant rain. But as you know, the winter of 2022/2023 turned out to be very warm, and in fact, only one week in January was downright cold and rainy.

Before winter there was still a beautiful, warm autumn, when pomegranates and oranges ripened.
