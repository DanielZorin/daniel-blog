Food

Let's look at a supermarket in Sweden. I usually shop at Lidl, a well-known discounter where prices are lower but there is less choice. In terms of prices in supermarkets, Sweden does not stand out as particularly expensive; prices are comparable to other Western European countries. Prices are shown in Swedish krona, so you can simply divide by 10 to convert to euros.
