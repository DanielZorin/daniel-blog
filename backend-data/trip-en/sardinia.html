Oristano

I crossed Sardinia from north to south by train on the main line. There are no special landmarks in the center of the island, so I just stopped in a more or less large city in the middle, called Oristano.

Landscapes of Sardinia. There are no high mountains here, unlike Sicily.

It’s really dry in the summer.

The main feature of Sardinia is the “nuraghe”. These are round towers.

There are almost 10 thousand such towers in Sardinia. They are left over from the civilization that existed here before the arrival of the Roman Empire. Their exact purpose is unknown.

Closer to the center of the island, the landscape becomes more hilly.

And nuraghe are more common.
