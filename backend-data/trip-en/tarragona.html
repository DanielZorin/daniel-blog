Spain: Tarragona, Salou, Barcelona

12-21 July 2011

Tarragona

Nice semi-resort Spanish town with Roman ruins.

The streets are similar to those in Barcelona.

National Catalan flags are hanging.

Drawings on the walls

Residential area.

Multi-colored columns.

Sundial.

Salou

The city is remembered for its embankment with palm trees.

And two-hour lines for every attraction at the local amusement park.

Barcelona
