China: Beijing

30 September - 22 October 2008

Beijing

In October 2008, I visited Asia for the first time - China. It was shortly after the 2008 Olympic Games, and I went to the World Mind Sport Games, which coincided with them. We lived in the Beijing Olympic Village, not far from the stadium.

China wasn't as high-tech then as it is now, but Beijing hasn't changed that much from 2008 to 2014. Only prices have increased significantly; in 2008 everything seemed ultra cheap to me (due to the exchange rate of the ruble, of course, too, but prices in yuan have also increased).
