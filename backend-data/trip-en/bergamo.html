Italy: Bergamo

30 November - 1 December 2016

Bergamo

The cheapest way to get from Cologne to St. Petersburg was to fly Ryanair Cologne-Milan-Tallinn. A ticket from Cologne to Milan cost only 8 euros, I think this is the cheapest of all my air flights (not counting free flight to St. Petersburg). I spent the night in a hostel in Milan near the train station because it was cheaper, but I spent the free half a day exploring the city of Bergamo, where the budget airport is located.

Incredible but true: the name of the city of Bergamo is pronounced with the emphasis on the first syllable.
