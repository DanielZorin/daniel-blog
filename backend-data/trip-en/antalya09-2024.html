Antalya

There were still a couple of interesting places in Antalya that I hadn't been to, since you can only get there by car. One day I had a car, and I needed to go to Antalya, which I took advantage of. The first place is the ruins of the ancient city of Termessos.

Termessos is located on the northwestern outskirts of Antalya, on the road to Korkuteli. From the main road, along which minibuses run, you need to go up the serpentine for another 10 kilometers.

Antalya is somewhere far below.

Then from the parking lot there is another rather long climb on foot.

And finally, the ruins of the ancient city on the hill!

Panorama from the entrance.
