Estonia: Tallinn

1-5 December 2016

Tallinn

On December 1, 2016, Alena’s birthday was celebrated in Tallinn, I had just flown there from the Caribbean.

At 11 pm, the establishments in the center were already closing, but the Dragon establishment on the main square was still open.

This touristy place is made in the style of a medieval tavern: a menu with several items, wooden cutlery, soup must be drunk from a bowl, the waitresses communicate in a familiar manner and call euros “gold coins”. Moreover, for such a tourist place it is not particularly expensive.

In winter there are few tourists in the evenings.

It's especially quiet on the hill.

Night panorama.

But during the day there are more people.

After Caribbean it was a little cold.

New Year's illumination.

City residents hang Christmas stars in their windows.

Photogenic liquor store.

Bar "Laboratory".

The menu is amazingly diverse - there are about 10 pages of cocktails like this.

Drinks are served in test tubes.

Prices. 10 shots cost from 10 to 22 euros, half as much at the beginning of the evening. It's expensive to buy one at a time.
