Turkey: Antalya

22-23 April 2024

Antalya

I had an early appointment on my next trip to Antalya, so I arrived the day before and booked a hotel. Thinking about what to see that I hadn't seen yet, I headed to the port to see Antalya from the water.

There are short cruises (about an hour) that cost a little over $10 (350 lira), and long ones for two hours, to the Duden waterfall, for 550 lira. But the long ones only go once a day, so I had to jump on the short one.
