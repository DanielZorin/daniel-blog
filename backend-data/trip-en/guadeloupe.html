Guadeloupe

5-8 May 2022

Guadeloupe, another French overseas territory, consists of two large islands separated by a very narrow strait, so that on the map it looks like one butterfly-shaped island. The islands are called "Grand Terre" (big land) - a flat island where most of the cities are, and "Basse Terre" (low land) - contrary to the name, a mountainous island, the center of which is occupied by a national park. The airport is located in the largest city of Pointe-à-Pitre. I rented a room right on the central square of the city, where the spice market operates during the day.
