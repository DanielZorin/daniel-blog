Malaysia: Kuala Lumpur, Malacca

11-14, 18-20 April, 2-3 May 2023

Kuala Lumpur

I haven't been to the eastern part of Asia for three years - for more than two years this part of the world was almost completely closed off due to Covid. I wanted to see the familiar beloved places, check how years without tourists had affected them, and eat durians and mangosteens. When I saw a two-way ticket from Istanbul to Kuala Lumpur for $475, I thought that was fateful.

In the three years that I have not been to Malaysia, a new metro line has been opened in Kuala Lumpur - Putrajaya Line. Another new line has already been drawn on the maps, it will mostly run in the suburbs of Klang and Shah Alam, although it will not open until 2024. Well, the Putrajaya line, as the name suggests, goes to the formal capital of Malaysia, but in a different way than the previously existing railway (Airport express). As usual, I went along the new line, photographing through the windshield.
