France: Strasbourg

20 January 2018

Strasbourg

The second time I came to Strasbourg was in January. The Christmas decorations were still there in some places, there was no snow, instead it rained most of the day.

The water level in the canals rose, so that the shops were flooded.

Last time I didn’t get to this dam, from which there is a good view of Petite France.
