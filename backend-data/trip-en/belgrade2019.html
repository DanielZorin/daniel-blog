Serbia: Belgrade

12 November 2019

Belgrade

I bought tickets to Paris on Serbian Airlines with a transfer in Belgrade for almost a day, so there was time to walk around the Serbian capital. The last time I was in Serbia in 2013, and six months later in Kosovo, which, according to part of the world, is also Serbia. Belgrade is still cheap and interesting.

It was my first time at Belgrade airport. It is quite close to the city center; the bus ride takes about 30 minutes. On the way, I saw an inscription on the wall that Kosovo is Serbia.

Some kind of slums.
