Russia: Saint Petersburg

7 February 2018

Saint Petersburg

I was in St. Petersburg for one day on the way from Estonia. It was unusually snowy that day.

The hotel right on Vosstaniya Square cost 600 rubles per day per room.

I went to the Finland Station and from there I started walking around the city.

Beautiful ice on the Neva.

A navigable piece is visible.

There is white all around.

The Fontanka is not frozen.

I looked into the mosaic courtyard - the last time I was here was also in winter, but there was almost no snow.

The summer garden looks a little ominous due to the fact that all the sculptures are hidden in wooden “coffins”.

Frozen Swan Canal.

Chinese tourists play snowballs on Dvortsovaya.

Here I turned away from the river.

The Griffins on Bankovsky Bridge were also locked in a box for repairs.

Sennaya. I bought some tangerines at the market.

The house where I lived in the fall of 2016.

Feodorovsky Cathedral.

Dinner at "Obed bufet" and then home.
