Turkey: Kadriye

24 December 2023

Kadriye

On Christmas Eve I visited the village of Kadriye, which belongs to the district (ilce) of the city of Serik, east of Antalya. This village is home to the Lang of Legends theme park.

Next to the park is a large open-air shopping center.

Pavilion.

Lock.

There are canals along which you can ride boats, just like in a Las Vegas casino.

Slide.

New Year decoration.

Christmas tree. The stall sold mulled wine, just like in Europe (and also for 4 euros).

Backlight.

The shopping center is strange in that there is no food court. There is only Starbucks and a couple other places with coffee and muffins, as well as two very expensive restaurants. I got out of this situation cleverly - I simply walked 15 minutes to the center of the village of Kadriye.

There are a lot of restaurants there with normal prices; you can eat Adana kebab for 170 liras.

I love Kadriye.

In the evening the shops are closed.
