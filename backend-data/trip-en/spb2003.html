Russia: St. Petersburg

May 2003

I went to St. Petersburg for the third time, again it was a prize for winning a school Olympiad, but this time the trip was short - only three days. This was shortly before the celebration of the city's 300th anniversary. We lived in the White Nights hotel in a poor, remote area of ​​​​Vesely Poselok behind the Neva (the nearest metro station is Lomonosovskaya).
