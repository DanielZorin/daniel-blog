Russia: Moscow

9 August 2017 - 10 March 2019

Moscow

In August 2017, I settled in Krasnogorsk near Moscow. Formally, I stopped being a tourist, but I still periodically made trips to Moscow in order to see all sorts of different areas where I had not been, and monuments. However, that fall I almost didn’t go anywhere; I only had photographs from just a couple of small hikes.
