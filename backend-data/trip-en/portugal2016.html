Porto

For the third time I’m in the most beautiful city on the planet, hurray!

What more can be said?

I walked along both bridges again.

Ruin under the second bridge.

I went to the gardens near the Crystal Palace, there is also an observation deck there.

I went across the river to where the port wine is stored.

On the main streets everything is the same.

Narrow descent to the embankment.

Bus station.

There is also plenty of graffiti here.

For the first time I tried a typical dish from Porto, called Francesinha. This is a multi-layer sandwich with fried eggs. I found a place where they sell it for 6 euros, although usually the price is around 8-10. This is a very filling sandwich, I would say that it's bigger than even the Big Tasty from Mac.
