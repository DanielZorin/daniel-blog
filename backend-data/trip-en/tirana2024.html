Albania: Tirana

19 August 2024

Tirana

Tirana is not the most obvious place for a transfer, but it was the best place to connect flights between Sicily and Antalya. I was in Tirana 10 years ago, during this time the city has changed beyond recognition!

I came by bus from the airport to the very center, there are some modern high-rises.

Oh no, there are old houses too.

They made a kitsch facade, that's Albanian.

Skanderbeg Square is now completely pedestrian!

The communist painting on the museum remained, everything around has changed.

The entire central area has been arranged as it should be.

Small streets are also pedestrian.

Mosque.

The central market is really hipster.

Bike paths.

A counter of passing cyclists. I watched - it works correctly.

Fountains.

The most epic: they remade the pyramid! It was opened after reconstruction in 2024.

Inside is now a hipster space.

And at the top is an observation deck.

Beauty.
