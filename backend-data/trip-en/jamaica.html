Jamaica: Kingston, Spanish Town, Montego Bay, Negril

14-19 May 2022

Kingston

Although Jamaica has a certain romantic Rastafarian aura, its crime rate ranks second in the world, exactly between El Salvador and Honduras. Crime is traditionally mainly concentrated in the capital of the country - the city of Kingston, so I explored this city with extreme caution. There weren’t even any airbnbs or cheap hotels in the city center, everything was further away from the seashore towards the hills - all because the center is a poor area, only the slums to the west are worse.

Spanish Town

Montego Bay

Montego Bay is the second city of Jamaica, located on the northwest coast. It has its own airport, which is much larger and more civilized than Kingston, since this is where 99% of tourists from the USA arrive. Also, cruise ships call at Montego Bay, not Kingston, so it's a very different Jamaica than the capital. Or not really?

The journey across the island was surprisingly fast, despite the old, crowded bus. To get to the northern coast, you need to drive through the pass.

I rented an apartment on the heights above the city (as usual, there was nothing in the center). A cruise ship is visible in the distance, a rare sight in the Caribbean these days.

Everything here is civilized, like in a rich area of ​​Kingston.
