Russia: Saint Petersburg

June 2002

Saint Petersburg

The second time I was in St. Petersburg, the trip was a prize for winning the school Olympiad. The trip was long, at least a week, and we visited many suburbs: Pavlovsk, Peterhof, Lomonosov, even Gatchina. We lived in the very center not far from Chernyshevskaya.

What's bad about the photos of the sights is that they make it seem like nothing has changed in the city in 20 years.

Except that the Mercedes van is outdated.

Opposite the Kazan Cathedral, the parking lot is not so hellish now.

And the toilets are still just as creepy.
