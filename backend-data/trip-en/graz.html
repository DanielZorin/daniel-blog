26-27 June 2023

Vienna

I haven’t been to Europe for quite some time (last time in the eastern part - Romania 2021, in the western part - England 2019), I wanted to see how things were there, walk along the pedestrian streets and ride trains instead of minibuses. I took tickets to where they were cheap, and now it’s June 2023, I’m in Vienna. More than 10 years have passed since last time.

A duck meets you at the airport. I crossed the border without any problems, as if nothing had happened.

I took the metro to the center and just walked along the streets towards the palace. Basically, I was in Vienna for the third time, and I didn’t have any special plans for this city. Just look at the main attractions one more time, and then go visit cities in Austria where I have not been.

Lienz

The Tyrol region is divided into three parts: north, south and east. Southern Tyrol after 1918 became part of Italy, and I will talk about it a little later. The northern and eastern ones remained part of Austria; they form the single state of Tyrol, but within Austria they do not border each other. The largest city in Tyrol is Innsbruck, but I was only in eastern Tyrol and its center, the city of Lienz.

The road to Lienz goes along a wide valley between two mountain walls.
