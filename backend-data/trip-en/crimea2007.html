Ukraine: Feodosia, Yalta

July 2007

Feodosia

The last time I went to Crimea was in 2007, that time with a camera. It was this time that I ventured outside of Feodosia, going on an excursion to Yalta. In general, Crimea, of course, deserves a more detailed visit; someday I need to return there.

The Feodosia beach really infuriated me. Every year there were more and more people there, so that in 2007 there was literally no free square meter. I didn’t take any photographs of it. Here is the Aivazovsky Museum in Feodosia.
