Turkey: Antalya

26 October 2022

Antalya

In October, my only trip was to Antalya. In addition to shopping, I finally went to the main museum in Antalya.

In the park between the Migros shopping center and the museum there is an alley of Turkish cinema stars.

Monuments.

Trees.

Showroom.

A film festival and an Oscar-like statuette.

Alley.

At the entrance to the museum there is a Lycian sarcophagus.

Above the entrance it is written approximately as follows: “The path to finding a homeland is to know the historical events that took place on this land, to understand the civilizations born here and pass them on {apparently to descendants}.”
