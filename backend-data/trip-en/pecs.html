Pecs

Pecs turned out to be one of the most interesting cities in Hungary besides Budapest. There is a lot of beautiful architecture in good condition and even ancient excavations. Perhaps I can recommend it on a par with Szeged.

The main local attraction is the early Christian burial grounds of the 4th-5th centuries, a World Heritage Site.

Inside are the remains of a church with seven choirs (septichora, hence the name).

But the main thing is the crypts with paintings.

It is organized very conveniently - you can look both from the side and from above, while everything is under glass, that is, the frescoes are protected from moisture.
