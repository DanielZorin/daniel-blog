Qatar

12-13 September 2024

Doha

I specifically bought a ticket with a long layover in Qatar so that I could sleep at night and fly the next day. Qatar is a convenient place for a layover, easy to get to the city, relatively inexpensive.

I booked a five-star hotel, which cost only about $40. I think it was the first time in my life that I stayed in a five-star hotel at my own expense.
