Sint Maarten

13-14 May 2022

To get from St. Kitts to Jamaica, located on the other side of the Caribbean Sea, I had two options: fly through Miami on a single ticket on American Airlines with a long layover and an overnight stay, or fly on two different airlines via Sint Maarten, also with an overnight stay. I chose the second option, since in Sint Maarten there was no need for a Covid test, which is needed in the USA and would have leveled the difference in price. I also found that spending the night in Sint Maarten was more comfortable and cheaper than in Miami, where I was only half a year ago. It turned out, however, that hotels in Sint Maarten have become more expensive since 2016.

The flight from St. Kitts to St. Maarten was on a small plane. First time in my life I flew in a cabin the size of a minibus.

Here I am at the famous airport.

I booked a room near the airport and got there in about 40 minutes on foot. The room is good, but 75 dollars is a bit expensive, of course, and on booking.com it’s generally twice as expensive (I booked on airbnb). On the French side there were rooms for around $50, but I would have to take a taxi there.
