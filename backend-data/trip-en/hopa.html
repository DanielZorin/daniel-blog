Turkey: Hopa

8 May 2014

Hopa

Hopa is a small town on the farthest north-eastern edge of Turkey, 20 kilometers from the border with Georgia. It is interesting mainly because Batumi airport is used as Hopa airport, i.e. you can buy a ticket from Istanbul to Hopa, and then you will be brought from Batumi airport to Hopa, bypassing Georgian passport control, and it will be cheaper than taking a ticket for the same flight, but to Batumi. Otherwise, the landscape here is similar to neighboring Adjara.
