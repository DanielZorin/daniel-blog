Ölüdeniz

The Lycian Way begins in Fethiye, and I was able to walk a small but one of its most beautiful sections. It starts in the village of Kayakoy in the mountains a few kilometers south of Fethiye.

Kayaköy is now a small village. You can get here by minibus once an hour.

But once it was much larger, and now most of the houses are destroyed.

The fact is that previously it was a village with a predominantly Greek population, but after the First World War and the creation of the Turkish Republic, there was an exchange of populations between Turkey and Greece, as a result of which the village was empty.

Now the whole large area on the hill is a picturesque ruin.
