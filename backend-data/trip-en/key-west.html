Key West

From Miami I went one day to Key West - a very unusual city. It is located on the island of the same name, 200 kilometers from the mainland, but you can get there by road along the chain of Florida Keys islands, connected by numerous bridges. The cheapest way to get to Key West is by excursion bus - it’s even cheaper than taking a regular bus, and it takes the same amount of time. I thought about renting a car, but abandoned this idea when I realized that spending the night anywhere on the islands from the mainland to Key West costs at least $200, even putting up a tent costs almost a hundred. Renting a car in Miami also cost ~$50 a day, and in one day I would just be busy driving back and forth, so a tour is much preferable.

The road to Key West itself is very interesting. You can see almost all the islands. The area near the coast of Florida is swampy; in the future, such landscapes will also be encountered.
