19-20 May 2022

Newark

Newark is a suburb of New York, located in the state of New Jersey just behind Jersey City. Newark is home to one of New York's three major airports. While looking for somewhere to stay for one night in New York, I discovered that Newark was much cheaper than, for example, the similarly remote outskirts of New York itself. Since I was flying into this airport, I decided to stay there, renting a room for only $35 (for comparison, a bed in the cheapest hostel in the city cost $70). In principle, the ride wasn’t even very far - 4 stops on the tram or half an hour on foot to the station 30 minutes to Manhattan by subway.

The center of Newark, as in a typical American city, is a block of high-rise buildings in the middle of low-rise buildings.

From the airport to downtown Newark you can take the train for $10 or the bus for $1.60. In general, travel in Newark costs 1.60, either by bus or by tram, and in New York it's 2.75, almost twice as expensive.

New York

My first trip to America began in New York, and my (possibly) last (for a long time) trip ends there. I don't really like New York and since then I've only been there once passing through, not staying for long on purpose. But in 2022, I specifically wanted to take a walk, because it is very symbolic.
