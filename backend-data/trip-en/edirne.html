Edirne

I had already visited the historical city of Edirne during my very first independent trip to Turkey. This time I spent more time in the city and explored it in more detail. Edirne (Adrianople) is the main city of Thrace, a part of Turkey located geographically in Europe. From here it is less than 20 kilometers to the borders with Bulgaria and Greece.

The main attraction - the Selimiye Mosque from the World Heritage List - was under restoration.

Tekirdag

Tekirdag is a regional center west of Istanbul on the shores of the Sea of ​​Marmara.

On the way to the town I discovered an unusual three-dimensional monument to Ataturk, which from the side looks like a set of stripes.

Shopping center in Tekirdag. The city is quite big.
