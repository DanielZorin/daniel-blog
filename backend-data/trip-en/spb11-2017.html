Russia: Saint Petersburg

21 November 2017

Saint Petersburg

I went to St. Petersburg for one day just like that, without a specific purpose.

I walked through the former industrial area next to the station on Ligovsky Prospekt.

This is where Bar 1703 is located, where Versus rap battles are filmed.

The writings on the walls give hints.

Two steps from here is the Ionoteka bar, where singer Grechka’s career began, and where RBL rap battles take place. Once upon a time there was an “Alpha Bar” in the same area, where #SlovoSPB battles took place, but it closed long ago.

Club "Mod", where the rap battle Gnoyny vs Ernesto took place.

Antihype.

The window of my once room.

I went to Apraksin Dvor to have lunch.

You can find authentic Chinese food here.

One dish costs 200-300 rubles

I bought some tangerines at the Sennaya market.

Next there was a small promenade along the sights.

I bought some cakes and bread from Volchek bakery to bring to Moscow.
