Slovakia: Trencin, Bratislava

4-5 December 2018

Trencin

The Slovak town of Trencin is interesting mainly for its medieval castle on a hill.

The oldest buildings here are from the 13th century (central tower). There are several levels of fortifications and bastions around it.

In Bratislava it is inexpensive, just like in the Czech Republic. The dish costs 4-5 euros.

Not bad at all.
