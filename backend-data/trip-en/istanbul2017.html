Turkey: Istanbul

3 April 2017

Istanbul

Istanbul is an excellent place for a transfer, many long-distance flights, no visa required, cheap, easy to get to the city. The last time I was in Turkey was at the beginning of 2014. Since then, considerable changes have occurred in the country: a coup attempt and a halving of the local currency (it fell even more in 2018). Portraits of the national leader hang at every step.

For one day, the route is more than standard: Hagia Sophia, Blue Mosque, etc., especially since Alena was in Istanbul for the first time.

Clothing rentals are available at the entrance to the Blue Mosque.

I didn’t go to the palace this time.

There are noticeably many tourists from Muslim countries in Istanbul, fortunately many of them do not need a visa to come to Turkey either.
