Russia: Tula, Yasnaya Polyana

13-14 October 2013

Tula

The main thing that catches your eye in Tula is that all the pillars in the city are painted in the colors of the Serbian flag.

Serbian flags are everywhere.

Tula is also the pride of Russia. And not only thanks to the obvious gingerbread, samovars and pistols.

For example, in Tula all public transport stops are signed (as in Cheboksary).

Even if the stop is a complete dump, overgrown with weeds.

At a major stop there is an electronic sign, Moscow nervously smokes on the sidelines.

In Tula, they managed to defeat advertisements for loans on a local scale.

There are bicycle parking areas in Tula.
